+2 Science Program

Uniglobe Secondary School's +2 Science program is a dynamic hub for aspiring scientists and critical thinkers. Students embark on a comprehensive journey that equips them with a solid foundation in Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics.

Modern classrooms, experienced instructors, and interactive labs foster analytical thinking and innovation. Through projects, discussions, and experiments, students develop scientific curiosity and real-world problem-solving abilities.
